Timeline FAQ Q: When should you make a timeline? A: If the plot is not continuing from the current timeline or derailing the main timeline plot massively, then a new timeline should definitely be made. Other than that, you can create one pretty much anytime. Q: Can one timeline affect another? A: Yes, but that doesn’t mean you should go crazy with timeline hopping. Q: Why multiple timelines? A: Some plots, like the death of everyone, just shouldn’t be permanently canon. Q: If someone time travels to a time before a timeline started and kills me, does the alternate timeline get affected? A: What? Q: Are there limitations to what the timelines can be? A: Nope.avi. The only limitations are obvious stuff like if it is NSFW or not. Q: When should I use #deleted-channel? A: When #main-timeline already has a session and you are starting or continuing a story in the 616 universe. This holds true for any 2nd channels added in the future. . (edited)
Voting FAQ Q: When should you make a vote? A: If you want something done which might be controversial, then you should make a vote. However, if it doesn't affect anyone in any objectively bad ways or doesn't need everyone's approval to be done, you can just ask Jonesy. If it doesn't even matter, like creating a new channel, then you can just do it yourself. Q: What type of votes can be done? A: The vote itself can be anything reasonable. The choices have to include at least one "Yes" and one "No" Q: What is "No" and what is "Yes"? A: "No" is "Nothing gets changed" and "Yes" is "Something gets changed". Q: How many need to vote for one option for it to be considered? A: More than half of the server has to vote for one option. If it's only half, the vote will be re-done, if it's less than half the votes for that option the option is not considered. With multiple choices, the most popular choice is considered, and a tie will result in a tie break where only the tied choices are voted for. Q: Can I change the amount needed for one option to be considered? A: No. Q: Can I vote on my own vote? A: Depends on the vote, but as of right now the only votes where you can vote on them yourself are votes on whether someone should join the server or not. Q: Why #voting and #voting-discussion? A: After a vote, it was decided that #voting is exclusively for making votes and documenting votes you are doing. Discussing the vote, saying your opinion, etc. can be done in #voting-discussion. Okay, onto the Q&A. Q: Can I make a new channel for a new book/tv show/video game? Don't know why you would need to ask that question, cause we all have admin perms here, but yeah!
